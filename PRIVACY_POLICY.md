# Privacy Policy / 隱私權政策

**Last updated / 最後更新日期:** 2025-12-03

## English

**Scoreboard - 匹克球、羽球萬用計分板** ("the App") is a utility application designed to help users keep score in various sports. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

### Information Collection and Use

**1. Personal Data**
The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. All scoring data is stored locally on your device.

**2. Third-Party Services (AdMob)**
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This may include:
*   Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
*   Usage data and diagnostics
*   Location data (if granted permission)

For more information on how Google AdMob collects and uses data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Data Security
We prioritize the security of your data. Since the App does not collect personal data on our servers, your scoring history remains on your device.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

### Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at james5ccccc@gmail.com.

Google AdMob がどのようにデータを収集および使用するかについての詳細は、Google のプライバシーポリシーをご参照ください：[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
